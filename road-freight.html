Reliable Road Freight Solutions

NEXGLOBE provides highly efficient and secure inland transportation services, connecting the UAE to the wider GCC region and beyond. Our road freight network ensures that your cargo reaches its final destination seamlessly.

We maintain a modern fleet of vehicles equipped to handle diverse cargo types. Whether you need Full Truck Load (FTL) for dedicated, direct deliveries, or Less than Truck Load (LTL) for consolidated, cost-effective transport, our dispatch teams ensure timely transit and strict safety standards.

  • ✔ FTL & LTL Capabilities: Solutions customized for your exact volume.
  • ✔ Cross-Border Expertise: Smooth transit across GCC borders.
  • ✔ Refrigerated Transport: Temperature-controlled vehicles for perishables and FMCG.
  • ✔ Last Mile Delivery: Ensuring safe delivery directly to your facility or clients.
